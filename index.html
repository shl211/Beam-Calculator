Beam Calculator

This is a beam calculator based on Engineer's Beam Theory, which is widely taught at undergraduate level in engineering degrees. A refresher of the theory can be found here. This tool is designed to provide a visually appealing alternative to experimental labs that students usually experience, which can be very monotonous due to small deflections. This tool is designed so that changing parameters will instantaneously be reflected in the visualisation. For those who want to use this tool to check solutions, this tool also provides diagrams and equations for deflections, shear force and bending moment.
